Our Amenities

Gated & Secured Boundary

Dream Heights prioritizes your peace of mind. Our secure living environment features a gated and secured boundary to keep you and your loved ones feeling safe.

Kids Play Area

Let your children create lasting memories in our dedicated kids' play area. Designed with their safety and enjoyment in mind, it's the perfect space for them to run free and socialize.

Ample Parking Space

No more circling for a spot! Dream Heights provides ample parking space, ensuring you can always find a convenient place to leave your vehicle.

Garden

Escape the hustle and bustle and unwind in our serene garden. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature and find a moment of peace amidst the landscaped greenery.

Gazebo For Senior Citizens

Designed for relaxation and socialization, our gazebo offers a comfortable space for senior citizens to gather with friends and neighbors.

Lift Facility

Dream Heights offers a lift facility, ensuring easy accessibility for all residents. Move around the building effortlessly and conveniently.

Club House

Unwind and connect with your neighbors in our well-equipped clubhouse. It's the perfect place to host gatherings, relax, or enjoy some friendly competition in the games room.

Spacious Bedrooms

Dream Heights offers spacious bedrooms, creating a comfortable and luxurious living experience. You'll have ample room to unwind, decorate, and create your own personal haven.
